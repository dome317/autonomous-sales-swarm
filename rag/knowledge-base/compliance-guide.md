# Relevant Compliance & Regulatory Guide

## GDPR in B2B Context

### Special Categories (Art. 9 GDPR)
- Personal data must be handled with care
- Processing only with explicit consent OR for contract fulfillment
- Data processing agreement (DPA) required with every service provider

### Business-specific Requirements
- Maintain records of processing activities
- Data protection impact assessment for new software
- Data protection officer required for organizations with 20+ employees handling personal data
- Mandatory breach notification: 72 hours

## Communication Regulations

### Business Communication Standards
- Advertising must be factual, not misleading
- Review requests are generally permitted when done professionally
- Cold outreach: Subject to opt-in requirements (UWG / ePrivacy)
- Transparency required for automated communications

### Company Types
- **Solo Business**: Single owner, full responsibility
- **Partnership**: Multiple equal partners
- **GmbH**: Limited liability company structure
- **Enterprise / Group**: Multiple locations, employed professionals

## Compliance Points for the Platform

1. **Proposal follow-up is legal**: Legitimate interest per Art. 6 (1) f GDPR
2. **Follow-up reminders need consent**: Marketing-like, Art. 6 (1) a GDPR
3. **WhatsApp only with consent**: Meta servers = third-country transfer
4. **Review requests are permitted**: Professional request is compliant
5. **Automated decisions**: Scoring must be transparent (Art. 22 GDPR)
