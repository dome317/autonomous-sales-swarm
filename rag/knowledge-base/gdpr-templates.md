## Data Processing — Legal Basis

| Purpose | Legal Basis | Note |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Appointment reminder | Art. 6 (1) b GDPR | Contract fulfillment |
| Proposal follow-up | Art. 6 (1) f GDPR | Legitimate interest |
| Follow-up reminders | Art. 6 (1) a GDPR | Consent required |
| Marketing | Art. 6 (1) a GDPR | Explicit consent |
| Review request | Art. 6 (1) f GDPR | Legitimate interest |

## Retention Periods

- Client master data: 10 years after last contact
- Communication history: 3 years
- Consent records: Duration of consent + 3 years
- Proposal data: 10 years (tax law)
