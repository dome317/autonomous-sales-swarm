# Product Features

## Core Functions

### Automated Proposal Follow-up
- Automatic follow-up reminders after proposals are sent
- Multi-step sequence: 3 days, 7 days, 14 days, 30 days
- Personalized messages based on service type and deal size
- Average proposal conversion rate increases from 42% to 67% (+25 percentage points)
- SMS, Email, and WhatsApp configurable as channels

### Digital Intake Forms
- Clients complete onboarding forms online before meetings
- Automatic import into CRM (multiple integrations supported)
- GDPR-compliant data storage on EU servers
- Multi-language: English, German, French, Spanish
- Saves an average of 8 minutes per client at reception

### Appointment Reminders & Follow-ups
- Automatic reminders: 48h, 24h, 2h before appointments
- Follow-up management for recurring services, check-ins, renewals
- No-show rate drops by 35% on average
- Clients can confirm or reschedule with one click

### Online Scheduling
- 24/7 booking via company website
- Calendar sync with CRM
- Intelligent time estimation
- 23% of online bookings are new clients

### Client Communication
- Two-way communication via WhatsApp Business
- Automated birthday greetings
- Service explanations as clear client info
- Post-service follow-up messages

### Review Management
- Automatic review requests after positive interactions
- Integration with Google Reviews, Trustpilot, G2
- Average +12 new 5-star reviews per month
- Negative reviews intercepted internally
