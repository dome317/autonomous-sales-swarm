# Case Study: TechStart GmbH, Berlin-Mitte

## Company Profile

- **Name**: TechStart GmbH
- **Location**: Berlin-Mitte
- **Team**: 3 consultants + 2 operations staff
- **Clients/Quarter**: ~1,200
- **CRM**: HubSpot
- **Focus**: SaaS consulting, implementation services

## Starting Situation

- Proposal acceptance rate: 41%
- Follow-up response rate: 28%
- No systematic proposal follow-up
- Operations team spent 4h/week on manual reminders
- 15% no-show rate for consultation calls

## Implementation

- **Month 1**: Professional plan setup, CRM integration
- **Month 2**: Proposal follow-up sequence activated, appointment reminders live
- **Month 3**: Follow-up system + online scheduling + review management

## Results after 6 Months

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Proposal acceptance rate | 41% | 68% | +27 PP |
| Standard service conversion | 55% | 74% | +19 PP |
| Follow-up response rate | 28% | 61% | +118% |
| No-show rate | 15% | 6% | -60% |
| New clients/month | 12 | 31 | +158% |
| Google Reviews | 3.8 (89) | 4.6 (147) | +0.8 stars |
| Admin time (operations) | 4h/week | 0.5h/week | -87% |

## Additional Revenue

- 27% more acceptances x 4,200 EUR = +68,000 EUR/quarter
- Product cost: 299 EUR/month = 897 EUR/quarter
- Net gain: +67,103 EUR/quarter
