# Case Study: Enterprise Solutions GmbH, Munich

## Company Profile

- **Name**: Enterprise Solutions GmbH
- **Location**: Munich-Schwabing, 2 offices
- **Team**: 12 consultants (Strategy, Operations, Technology, HR, Finance)
- **Clients/Quarter**: ~4,800
- **CRM**: Salesforce
- **Focus**: Full-service consulting, premium segment

## Starting Situation

- Proposal acceptance rate overall: 38%
- Premium service proposals: Only 26% acceptance (high values, long decision time)
- No unified follow-up system across both offices
- 3 full-time operations staff dedicated to client communication
- Client complaints about lack of information after initial consultations

## Implementation

- **Month 1**: Enterprise setup, Salesforce integration for both offices
- **Month 2**: Service-specific follow-up sequences (5 different types)
- **Month 3**: Custom API integration with internal client portal
- **Month 4**: Complete follow-up system + review automation

## Results after 12 Months

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Proposal acceptance rate (overall) | 38% | 64% | +26 PP |
| Premium service acceptance | 26% | 51% | +25 PP |
| Average decision time | 24 days | 9 days | -63% |
| Follow-up response rate | 31% | 55% | +77% |
| No-show rate | 11% | 5% | -55% |
| New clients/month | 45 | 78 | +73% |
| NPS (Net Promoter Score) | 34 | 71 | +109% |
| Operations admin hours/week | 120h | 35h | -71% |

## Additional Revenue

- Premium services alone: +25 PP x ~30 proposals/month x 8,500 EUR = +63,750 EUR/month
- Total additional revenue across all services: +185,000 EUR/month
- Enterprise plan: 499 EUR/month
- ROI: 37,074%

## Key Successes

1. **Financing integration**: Automatically sends payment plan info for proposals > 3,000 EUR — acceptance rate for these proposals increased by 34%
2. **Cross-office reporting**: Single dashboard view for both locations
3. **Staff reallocation**: 2 of 3 operations positions redirected to value-generating tasks

## Quote
